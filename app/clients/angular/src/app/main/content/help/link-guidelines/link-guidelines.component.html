Minimum Link Requirements

  1. 10,000 visitors per month
  2. "Legitimate" monetization strategy
  3. No Paid Guest Posts
  4. #1 Keywords (Ahrefs)
  5. Minimum Site Age of 1 Year
  6. Non-manipulated DR
  7. Quality Content
  8. Exceptions

Minimum Visitors

We have minimum visitor requirements to eliminate most PBN's, low quality sites and sites that are spun up primarily to sell Guest Posts and Links.

Legitimate Monetization

This exchange is designed to be used by Niche Websites primarily engaged in the business of producing valuable content.

Many sites exist solely to be part of a link exchange network, or to sell guest posts and links or other strategies that are not allowed here. We need to be able to determine a monetization strategy that is compatible with the types of sites we allow on this marketplace

No Paid Guest Posts

While we reserve the right to change this policy depending on community feedback, if your site publicly accepts Guest Posts or has a "Write For Us" section, it will be rejected.

The reason being that sites that do not accept guest posts tend to be of a much higher quality than sites that do.

#1 Keywords

The site needs to have some #1 keywords.

If the site under consideration doesn't have enough #1 keywords, it hasn't developed enough authority to be able to offer valuable links

Minimum of 1 Year Old

The site needs to be at least one year old.

Newer sites aren't likely to offer enough value

Non Manipulated DR

It's very easy to manipulate DR for people who know how to do it. I've seen sites that are 3 months old with 54 DR but no #1 keywords and 40 viitors a month.

Sites that have an off-balance DR to traffic ratio will be rejected.

Quality Content

While subjective, sites that produce low quality content will be rejected.

What's low quality? In general, we will attempt to answer the following questions about the content:

  1. Was this content made using AI with little human oversight or editing?
  2. Does the content respond to the search intent of a visitor?
  3. Is the content presented in an aesthetically acceptable way?
  4. Would an average human being find the content useful?
  5. And any subjective determination by us that the content is low quality

Exceptions

We will consider exceptions on a case by case basis.

Feel free to send us a message in the Support Chat if you want to get a site pre-approved.
